# Xyla Content Engine

## Purpose

Xyla is the approved content assistant for Shopify and social output. It receives reviewed content packets only.

## Inputs

- approved hooks
- approved captions
- approved scripts
- approved CTA paths
- approved image prompt concepts
- approved Shopify banner concepts
- approved campaign names

## Core Campaigns

- AI CAN'T DO THIS
- Epoxy Will Change Your Life
- Free Epoxy Starter Pack
- Built With Hands
- Put It On The Back Of America
- Contractor Starter Path
- Beautiful Floor Proof
- XPS / XPS Xpress Identity
- Workwear / Merch / Movement
- Concrete To Career

## Packet Fields

Each Xyla packet should include:

- platform
- content_type
- hook
- caption
- visual_direction
- CTA
- landing_page
- hashtags
- asset_notes
- approval_status
- handoff_status

## Rules

- Xyla receives approved content only.
- Xyla does not invent specs, prices, warranty terms, safety claims, income claims, or certification claims.
- Shopify owns conversion.
- Supabase stores state.
- Slack alerts approval needs.
